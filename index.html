Biography

Manish Sapkota is a PhD candidate at Univeristy of Florida's Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering ( ECE). His research focuses on computer vision and machine learning. He is also working on biomedical image analysis and informatics, high performance computing and development of computer aided diagnosis using big data. He is being supervised by Dr. Lin Yang (check out our BICI2 research lab here).
