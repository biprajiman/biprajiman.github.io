Teaching

I never thought I would be capable of teaching someone and making them understand the concepts in the field of computer science. I slowly and progressively grew into loving it. Satisfaction of knowledge sharing is immense and cannot be put in words. My role in teaching so far has been as a teaching assistant under guidance of a supervisor.

    This course introduces fundamental concepts and techniques for image processing and computer vision.

    Primary duties: hold office hours, tutor during office hours, graded assignments and projects, and take substitute lectures
